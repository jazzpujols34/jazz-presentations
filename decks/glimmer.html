Glimmer

AI-powered memorial video generation. Transform photos into cinematic moments — entirely in the browser.

glimmer.video

The Problem

Memorial videos cost thousands
and take weeks to produce

  • Professional video production: NT$15,000 – NT$50,000+
  • Turnaround: 1–3 weeks, often too late for the ceremony
  • DIY tools require video editing expertise most families don't have
  • AI video generation exists, but no product stitches it into a final film

The Solution

Upload photos. Get a film.

Glimmer transforms your photos into cinematic AI-generated video clips. Select an occasion — memorial, birthday, wedding, or pet tribute — and our multi-provider AI engine generates professional video from each photo with category-aware prompts.

Then edit everything in a browser-based timeline editor with music, subtitles, filters, and title cards. Export the final MP4 right from your browser.

Architecture

Edge-Native. Zero servers.

Browser Next.js 16 + FFmpeg WASM
Edge Cloudflare Pages · 43 API routes
Storage KV for state + R2 for videos
AI Providers BytePlus Seedance · Google Veo · Kling AI

Client-Driven Polling

POST /generate returns a job ID. The browser polls GET /status/[id] — each request checks the external AI provider and updates KV. On completion, videos auto-archive to R2.

Edge-First Design

All 43 API routes run on Cloudflare Edge Runtime. No Node.js server. FFmpeg WASM handles video export entirely in the browser.

Multi-Provider Failover

Smart dispatch across BytePlus, Google Veo, and Kling AI. Provider selection is category-aware — person vs. pet, occasion-specific prompts.
